The results of our trading for the nine months from 01.04 - 30.12.2019

We all welcome and congratulate you on the new year 2020!

Dear friends, it has been nine months since the opening of our TELEGRAM channel Forexindexed. It's time to take stock. All this time we have been writing honestly and openly, and most importantly in a timely manner, about our deals and results. For 2020, we have reserved several positions, and to sum up the results for 2019, we have re-evaluated the positions as of December 30, 2019. Therefore, in the new year we will enter with open positions that will be opened at the market closing prices as of 30.12.2019 . Starting from the New year of 2020, we will start a new countdown of transactions and financial results for transactions . As in the previous year of 2019, in the New year we will expect a yield close to 60% per annum on our operations, we believe that it is quite realistic to do this using a maximum of the second leverage on the capital for each instrument. The end of 2019 was not entirely successful for us, as we were unable to make money on oil operations since mid-November and suffered losses, including on stock indices. At the same time, our long positions are in precious metals metals have not yet brought us the expected returns, although they have become profitable.

So, for 9 months or 39 weeks , 617 deals were completed, 277 deals were closed at a loss , and 340 at a profit . We don't we set ourselves the task of catching every movement of the market, performing a huge number of operations, and try to open transactions only on those patterns that seem to us the most optimal and potentially bring profit with greater probability. The largest weekly loss, for all the time of operation, we had was equal to -15.07% on the capital and it happened in mid-November. The most profitable week brought us 26.23% on capital in the second half of September. Of the 9 months of our public trading, we ended the last 2 months of 2019 with a loss, and in the first 7 months our result has always remained positive, which is reflected in the chart. In December, our main income was generated by operations in the foreign exchange market on the market and investments in gold and silver, and on operations with oil and stock indices, we received a loss.
