We estimate the performance of Polyus-Gold for the 1st quarter of 2018.

In this article, we will analyze the report for the 1st quarter of the company"Polyus Gold" and make brief conclusions: whether it is worth buying the company in your portfolio and if so, at what prices."

Pole it belongs to one of the largest gold mining companies in the world and Russia. The company sells gold mainly to banks. We are very interested in this asset because we know that a part of our investments should be in gold, preferably 5-7%, as it is a hedge against any economic and political crises of the world level. During the crisis, its cost can grow 2-3 times. But when everything is quiet, gold can make losses. Investing in a high-quality gold mining company eliminates these disadvantages.

When everything is good in the market, the company will feel good too, and because it pays dividends, then we we receive investments in gold with the effect of a Bank Deposit.

As we can see in the moments of crisis in 2008, the price rose very much. Now it is in a sideways movement, but at a rather attractive level at the company level.

If there was any overbought in the shares, the market has long ago removed it. The price has fallen by 25% from its highs.

Let's move on to report:

We can see from the report that revenue grew by 1%, i.e. there is no growth here. Production increased by 13%, but sales fell by 6%, but due to rising costs the company's products managed to increase revenue. You can't call the report a breakthrough, but rather it's neutral.

How we see that the company's debt burden continues to decline slightly and the company's stability continues to increase. This is a positive development. Anything less than a ratio of 2.5 will allow the company to pay good dividends for its industry. This is 30% of EBITDA, but not less than 550 million dollars.

Subtotal:

The company is trading at quite attractive levels taking into account the dividend policy and anything around 4,000 rubles per share is an attractive price to buy. Due to good gold prices and the weakening of the ruble + there were no sold-out stocks since Q1, and we expect good figures in the second quarter. The company pays dividends 2 times a year at the level of 251 rubles per year. At the current cost, we get 6.4% per annum+ growing business and protection from crises. I hold this asset and will bribe in case of any drawdowns up to 7 % of my own portfolio's.

After all, the situation in emerging markets is now very turbulent.
