What industries should you invest in during the crisis?

What industries should you invest in during the crisis?

This year, the global financial markets are experiencing very severe shocks. A lot of unexpected things happened in less than six months. Let me remind you that the year started with great fears about the war in the middle East, but now the problems are completely different. Combination negative factors led to a strong correction, although the lost positions were partially restored.

For example, the bond market was able to recoup a 20% drop in just three weeks. Now debt securities are at a historic high. The current time is very difficult for investors, because it is difficult to decide on actions and many people think that the moment for investing has already passed.

At a time of turbulence in the markets, it is better to choose long-term investments, even better if your portfolio is index-based. This will increase diversification and reduce desire change something in your portfolio.

However, if you prefer to invest in individual securities, then you can pay attention to the following industries. Please note that this material is not an investment recommendation.

Let's take a look at a few industries that have not yet been able to return to the market. pre-crisis values.

1. Airlines. Due to the closure of borders, many air carriers lost more than 60% in price. For American airlines, Buffett's Pro statement was a blow lack of growth potential. At the moment, the share price of the leading airlines is equal to the share price of ZOOM.

2. Oil companies. Companies that produce black gold have been able to diversify their businesses very much over their long history: they have their own media projects and financial structures and IT companies. Anyway, the company is too big to just "die".

3. Chain hotels . Modern business models are very much changing the real estate market. AIRBNB has changed the rental market. This statement is correct for travel, but what about employee accommodation during travel business trips? Most large companies have a contract with chain hotels, so large hotel chains will still be in demand. However, it is necessary to open borders in order to resume business.
