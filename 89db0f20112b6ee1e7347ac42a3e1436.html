iPhone 11 (Giveaway)

iPhone 11 is the last product of Apple Company in the iPhone series. There are no major changes in the design of this new phone and fans can expect the classic style which is quite simple yet effective. According to the news, new models will be available in four colors. These colors are Gold, Space Gray, Silver, Night Green. Two different models will be launched at the same time as the iPhone 11 Pro and iPhone 11 Pro Max and as usual, Max model is larger than the other model. Apple decided to not launch models with 32GB anymore. The storage capacity of new models is 64, 256 and 512GB. Models will use A13 bionic chip which is a third-generation Neural Engine. This will allow the phones to run faster. Models are 4 hours more durable in terms of the battery life when compared to the previous model, iPhone XS. The max model is one hour more durable than the regular model. Apart from these, most of the other features are the same but they are optimized for better personal use and quicker run.
